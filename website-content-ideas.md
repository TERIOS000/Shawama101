# Business Website Content Ideas

Use this as a simple guide for what to add next to each page of the website.

## Home Page

The home page should quickly explain what the business does.

Add:

- Business name
- Short welcome message
- Main products or services
- A short reason customers should choose this business
- Button or link to contact page

Example:

> Welcome to Paiboon Phatthanakit Selaphum. We provide quality products and reliable service for customers in our area.

## Brands Page

The brands page should show what brands or product lines the business sells.

Add:

- Brand names
- Brand logos
- Product categories
- Short description of each brand
- Photos of popular products

Example sections:

- Construction materials
- Home supplies
- Tools and equipment
- Featured brands

## Gallery Page

The gallery page should help customers trust the business by showing real photos.

Add:

- Product photos
- Shop photos
- Customer project photos
- Delivery or service photos
- Short captions under each image

Example captions:

- Products available in store
- Recent customer order
- Shop front
- Delivery service

## About Us Page

The about page should tell the story of the business.

Add:

- When the business started
- What the business specializes in
- Where the business is located
- What makes the business reliable
- Business values, such as quality, honesty, and good service

Example:

> Our business has served customers in Selaphum with quality products, fair prices, and friendly service.

## Contact Page

The contact page should make it easy for customers to reach the business.

Add:

- Phone number
- Address
- Opening hours
- Facebook page
- Line ID
- Google Maps link

Example:

```text
Phone: 000-000-0000
Open: Monday - Saturday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Location: Selaphum, Roi Et
```
